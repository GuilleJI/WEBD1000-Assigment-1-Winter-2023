Race information

The 2021 Diane's Run will be held Sunday, September 12, in Cheyenne starting at Prairie Park. All race fees are non-refundable and non-transferable, even in the event of inclement weather. Two races are offered: a 5K (3.1 miles) and a 10K (6.2 miles) event. We also offer a 1-mile Family Fun Walk.
